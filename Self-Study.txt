  Semantic html gives meaning to a browser or search engine. It allows browsers or search engines to know what roles are being played in the page.

Name two big differences between display: block; and display: inline;.

  Display: block retains height and width, it also takes up as much space as its container allows. Furthermore it disrupts the content flow.

  Display: inline doesn't affect the content flow. It does not retain a height or width. It only takes up the space that the content allows.
